    Military to Civilian (MCT) - Digital Transition Centre

    Welcome to the Digital Transition Centre! This is your central location to plan and complete your transition from the military to post-service life. Not sure where to start? Book a consultation with a Transition Advisor.

    Services and information

    Transition Options within the CAF

    Explore career change options in the CAF that could work for you. The CAF has over 150 different occupations, as well as paid education for some of those occupations, that may be of interest to you. Click here to book an appointment with a Base Personnel Selection Officer to explore your options.

    Translate your military skills, training, and experience to civilian equivalencies

    The skills, training and experiences you've accumulated during your military career are highly sought after equivalencies for civilian jobs. Learn more here.

    Locate your closest Transition Centre

    There are 32 Transition Centres across the country where members and their families can seek information, services, and support from Military to Civilian (MCT) transition subject-matter experts.

    Take transition training

    Take transition training to ensure you have the knowledge, skills, and resources to support continued and continually improving well-being throughout the transition process and beyond.
