    Take transition training

    Military to civilian transition is more involved than just leaving a job - it is, for many of us, a significant life change. As part of our efforts to serve members who have dedicated their efforts to serving Canada as they return to civilian life, training is in place to ensure each is ready for their next mission. The training will provide you with the knowledge, skills, and resources to support continued and continually improving well-being throughout your transition process and beyond.
