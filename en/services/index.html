    CAF Transition Portal

    For Use by Military Members Only

    This service is strictly for use by Canadian Armed Forces personnel. If you are a veteran of the Canadian Armed Forces, please use My VAC Account service provided by Veterans Affairs Canada.

    Welcome to the CAF Transition Portal, the best way to initiate or continue your transition process.

    BEFORE SIGNING IN FOR THE FIRST TIME, PLEASE READ THE FOLLOWING IMPORTANT INFORMATION:

    ✔ Ready to initiate your transition process
    Logging into this portal is only required if you wish to start your transition process at this time. This portal allows you to initiate your transition support process, an important step to help you make the best possible decision about your future within the CAF and much more.
    ✔ Initial communication from your Transition Centre
    To provide you with the best possible transition support, upon logging into this transition portal, you will receive an initial communication from your local Transition Centre.
    ✔ Security and information sharing
    Your information is protected in a secure, controlled-access digital environment and is shared only with your local Transition Centre.
